# my data dictionary
## ID
**Description:** the idem's number.
## Title
**Description:** the electronic literature's name.
## Author
**Description:** the author name of the electronic literature.
## Year
**Description:** publication date of the work. 
## tpye
**Description:** The “genre” or “literary form” of electronic literary works.  
**Allowed values:** hypertext, generative, interactive fiction, fiction, kinetic poetry, multimedia, comics,game.
## Access_data
**Description:** The time I visited these websites.
## URL
**Description:** the work's website link.
## Technology
**Description:** The technical medium or technical mechanism of this electronic literary work.  
**Allowed values:** HTML, Javascript, Audio-visual web platform, web multimedia, web-based.
## interaction_level
**Description:** Degree of reader interaction required.  
**Allowed values:** none, low, medium, high.
## Textuality
**Description:** The composition of the text.  
**Allowed values:** linear, non linear, modular, database-like.
## Versioning
**Description:** the work versions' condition.  
**Allowed values:** stable, updated, multiple versions, unclear.
## Language
**Description:** the work's language.
## Metadata_source
**Description:** the work's source.
## Preservation_status
**Description:** The preservation status of the work and whether it is still usable.  
**Allowed values:** live, archived, partially broken, inaccessible.
## License
**Description:** The license statement of the work.  
**Allowed values:** CC BY-NC-ND 2.5, CC BY-SA 4.0, no stated.
## one_sentence_description
**Description:** The summary of the works.
